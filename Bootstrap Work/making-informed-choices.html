Making Nutrition Information Accessible

At McDonald’s® Canada, it’s been our long-standing practice to provide relevant nutrition information so you can make informed food choices. Our Nutrition Calculator allows you to view the nutrition profile for your favourite menu items, and customize them to suit your specific needs by subtracting ingredients. You can also get nutrition info on the go by downloading the free MyMcD’s App. Just tap on a menu item to view its complete nutrition and ingredient information.
